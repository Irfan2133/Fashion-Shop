about us

Raiya Tex is recognized as one of the reputed apparel and well-established Export-oriented manufacturing, sourcing agent and Inspection Company. We are situated in the famous commercial area at Uttara Dhaka in Bangladesh. The company has been established in 2012.

Raiya Tex is working as clothing manufacturing and buying sourcing with many countries buyers such as for USA, Poland, Brazil, U.K.,German and French, etc.
